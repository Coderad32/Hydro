Sigil Matrix definitions

  • Exclamation (!): Represents logical negation.
  • At (@): Used to denote arrays.
  • Hash (#): Indicates comments in Perl code.
  • Dollar ($): Represents scalar variables.
  • Percent (%): Used for hashes.
  • Caret (^): Represents the bitwise XOR operator.
  • Ampersand (&): Used for subroutine calls.
  • Asterisk (*): Represents dereferencing.
  • Left Parenthesis ((): Used for grouping expressions.
  • Right Parenthesis ()): Closes a grouping expression.
